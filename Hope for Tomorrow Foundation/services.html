Our Services

At the Hope for Tomorrow Foundation, our work is centered on breaking cycles of poverty and creating opportunities for underprivileged children in Gautengâ€™s townships and rural areas. Since our establishment in 2018, we have designed programs that not only address immediate educational needs but also build long-term pathways for success.

1. Digital Literacy Workshops

We run digital literacy workshops to equip learners with essential computer and technology skills. In a world driven by technology, access to digital knowledge helps children prepare for the modern workplace and gives them the tools to succeed in school and beyond.

2. High School Scholarships (Grades 10-12)

Through our scholarship program, we provide financial support for learners in Grades 10-12. These scholarships give deserving students the chance to complete their high school education and pursue postsecondary studies or career opportunities.

3. After-School Tutoring

Our after-school tutoring program provides additional support in core subjects:

  • Afrikaans
  • English
  • Mathematics

These sessions are led by volunteer tutors ranging from college students to retired teachers, ensuring that every child has the academic support they need to thrive.

Our Impact

  • Partnered with 15 schools in Gauteng.
  • Recruited 150 volunteer tutors.
  • Assisted over 2,500 children in communities such as Soweto, Alexandra, and Katlehong.
